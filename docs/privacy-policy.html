Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

LazyFill ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal data.

LazyFill does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

All data (such as saved profiles and form inputs) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Your data never leaves your device.

3. How Data is Used

The stored data is used only to enable autofill functionality across websites. It helps you quickly fill forms using saved profiles.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties.

5. Permissions Explanation

  • storage: Saves your profiles locally
  • activeTab: Access current page for autofill
  • host permissions: Allows operation across websites

6. Security

Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, users maintain full control over their information.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
