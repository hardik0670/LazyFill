Privacy Policy for LazyFill

Effective Date: [Add Date]

LazyFill ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

LazyFill does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

All user data, including saved profiles and form information, is stored locally within your browser using Chrome's storage API.

This data remains entirely on your device and is never shared with any third parties.

3. Data Usage

The data stored locally is used solely to provide the autofill functionality of the extension, enabling users to quickly fill web forms.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. No user data is transmitted outside your browser.

5. Permissions Justification

  • storage: Used to store user profiles locally.
  • activeTab: Allows interaction with the current webpage to fill forms.
  • scripting: Enables form detection and autofill functionality.
  • host_permissions: Required to operate across websites for autofill.

6. Security

Since all data is stored locally and not transmitted, the risk of data exposure is minimized. Users maintain full control over their information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
